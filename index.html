Rules for Sustainable Saga

- If you take the litter to the right dustbin, your score will increase by +1.
- If you take the litter to the wrong dustbin, your score will decrease by -1.
- The litter will come from below and move upward before going down again.
- You will have a timer to put sssthe litter in the dustbin.
- Have fun and play sustainably!
